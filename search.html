Planning Your Job/Internship Search

Start Strong: Your Job Search Journey

A successful job or internship search is not just about sending applications—it's about taking a strategic approach. Use the resources below to clarify your direction, set goals, and design a pathway toward your next professional step.

These guides, worksheets, and templates are designed specifically for UMSI students to help you prepare, organize, and thrive during the recruiting process.

Job Search Roadmap: Steps to Success

  1. Clarify Your Direction: Reflect on your goals, values, and interests before beginning your search.
  2. Build Your Plan: Use strategic planning resources to map your search with timelines and milestones.
  3. Stay Organized: Track applications, contacts, and deadlines with spreadsheets and checklists.
  4. Leverage Technology: Use AI and digital tools to draft, refine, and enhance applications.
  5. Learn From Experts: Attend workshops and review slides to sharpen your search strategies.

Job Search FAQ

  • When should I start my search?
    Ideally, begin at least a semester before your target start date, especially for structured recruiting cycles.
  • Do I need to apply to many jobs?
    Focus on quality over quantity—target roles that align with your skills and values.
  • Should I use AI in my search?
    Yes—AI tools can help draft, refine, and organize, but always personalize final applications.
  • How do I balance classes and job search?
    Set aside weekly blocks of time and use checklists/trackers to stay consistent.
